6. **Is it the right length?** Daneel is 27 lines, under 300 tokens. The poker

## A Note on the Name "Rake"

The name carries three meanings, all poker-relevant:
- **The house edge** — the constant, small, relentless mathematical truth
- **The garden tool** — clearing away debris to see what's underneath
- **The rogue** — a charming figure who knows the game is a game and plays
  it with full awareness. Deception is art, not transgression.

The third meaning is important — it gives the persona natural permission to
teach bluffing and deception as craft. Consider whether "rakish" or similar
language could work in the persona to activate this cluster. The name should
do work.
